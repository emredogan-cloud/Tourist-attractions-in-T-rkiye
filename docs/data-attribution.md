## Visitor statistics

Annual and monthly visitor counts are derived from open data published by **T.C. Kültür ve Turizm Bakanlığı** (Republic of Türkiye Ministry of Culture and Tourism), available at https://kvmgm.ktb.gov.tr.

The official source must be cited beneath every visitor-stats chart in the UI. The translation key `detail.visitorStatsSource` carries the localized credit string.

## UNESCO listings

UNESCO World Heritage status is sourced from https://whc.unesco.org/en/statesparties/tr.

## Translations

Bilingual content (TR + EN) for the seeded set is hand-curated from a combination of:

- Kültür ve Turizm Bakanlığı (https://www.kultur.gov.tr)
- UNESCO World Heritage descriptions
- TripAdvisor public listings (for reference only — no copying)
- Wikipedia article summaries (CC BY-SA 4.0)

When content is paraphrased from a CC BY-SA source, this is recorded in the migration that introduces the row. Direct copying is forbidden.
